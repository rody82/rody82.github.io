Controls

  • Up - Accelerate
  • Down - Break and Reverse
  • Left/Right - Turn the car
  • Space - Handbrake
  • Enter - Confirm, use Hotel, start/stop the ferry boat
  • Tab - Minimap
  • Esc - Exit
  • R - Restart level / reset high scores
  • D - Destruct car
  • F - Show/hide fps (22 is full speed)
  • H - Honk
  • P - Pause

  • Replay: select a slot on the high scores screen with the up/down arrows
  • Reset high scores: press R on the high scores screen
